## 🧠 Key Learnings

## 🔴 Core Problem
- SSH open to the world creates immediate exposure risk

## 🧠 Key Insight
- Public subnet does NOT mean safe or controlled
- It only means internet-reachable

## 💥 Attack Reality
- Attackers continuously scan public IP ranges
- Misconfigured SSH is a common entry point

## ⚠️ Critical Lesson
Security is about limiting exposure, not just deploying infrastructure

## 🧾 Final Conclusion
Overexposed networks are one of the fastest ways to get compromised in cloud environments.
